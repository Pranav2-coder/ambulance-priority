About the Project

Our Smart Traffic Light system uses IoT-based sensors to detect emergency vehicles and activate a white priority signal, ensuring faster and safer travel for emergency responders.

The system adjusts dynamically, prioritizing safety and reducing congestion with the help of cloud-powered automation and real-time microcontroller logic. By creating intelligent intersections, we're building safer cities one traffic light at a time.
